To realize our shared vision of Freedom, we must radically reimagine our economic systems. The Alignment Economy represents not just an evolution of capitalism, but a paradigm shift in how we understand value, progress, and the very purpose of economic activity.

At its core, the Alignment Economy seeks to harmonize our eco-nomic choices with our highest aspirations, creating a system where prosperity and purpose go hand in hand. This framework provides a revolutionary reinterpretation of money’s role. Tradi-tionally, money has served as a medium of exchange, a unit of account, and a store of value. In the Alignment Economy, we ex-pand upon these functions, transforming money into a tool for measuring and incentivizing actions that align with our collective pursuit of Freedom.

In this new paradigm, prices evolve beyond mere indicators of scarcity or demand. They become dynamic signals reflecting the degree of alignment between individual actions and our shared goals. Money transforms into a mechanism of bounded autono-my, allowing individuals to diverge from the collective will while measuring and limiting that deviation. This approach creates a dynamic feedback loop, continually guiding economic activity towards outcomes that enhance our collective well-being.

Building upon Kant’s categorical imperative—which posits that an action is morally right if it could be universally applicable without contradiction —we propose a model that rewards actions aligned with our collective pursuit of Freedom. For instance, consider the price of producing a product that involves environmental degradation. In the Alignment Economy, an AGI system could aggregate human values, scientific data, and long-term impact assessments to quantify the degree of discord such actions represent.
