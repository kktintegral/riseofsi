Sustainable Design and Lifecycle Management: When creating physical embodiments of AI, such as robots or smart devices, we can emphasize ease of creation, repair, and recycling. This approach ensures that AI-enabled technologies are designed with sustainability in mind from the outset. It involves adopting circular economy principles in AI hardware development, using recyclable materials, designing for easy disassembly and repair, and creating systems for efficient recycling of AI components at the end of their life cycle.
