Recursive Self-Improvement: As AGI systems gain the capacity for recursive self-improvement, their potential impact on soci-ety magnifies. Freedom’s emphasis on prosocial values and its adaptable nature provide a robust framework for guiding this rapid technological advancement. It aims to ensure that, as AGI capabilities grow exponentially, they remain increasingly aligned with core human values. However, due to the com-plexities involved in recursive self-improvement, there is no guarantee that this alignment will be perfectly maintained at all stages. Continuous oversight and adaptation are essential to prevent AGI systems from pursuing narrow objectives that could undermine broader human and ecological well-being.

Freedom is the universal objective function for AGI, one which naturally combines with local preferences. By integrating the AI alignment framework into the design of the Alignment Economy, we can create an economic system where AGI acts as a steward of human values, guiding both human and machine decisions towards a more just, sustainable, and fulfilling future.

A Flywheel of Progress The Alignment Economy is not a static blueprint but is a dynamic growing framework designed to evolve alongside our understanding of ethics, our technological capabilities, and our aspirations for the future.

This evolutionary approach recognizes that:

Values Evolve: Our understanding of what is “right” and “good” is constantly evolving. The Alignment Economy, through continuous dialogue and adaptation, can reflect these evolving values in its price signals, ensuring that the economy remains aligned with our moral compass. This requires ongoing public discourse and mechanisms for incorporating new ethical insights into economic structures.

Humanity Progresses: The pursuit of Freedom is an ongoing journey, a continuous striving towards a better future.
