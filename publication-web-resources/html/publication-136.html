A Future Rooted in Meaning The future cannot simply abandon the past, for its continuation provides identity. Severing ourselves from the richness of historical experience would strip AGI of its deeper purpose. Our history, our struggles, our triumphs—all of these provide the narrative context within which AGI will operate. Just as our limbic system provides the emotional essence that our cortex contextualizes, so too will human experience provide the moral and emotional essence for AGI’s otherwise abstract intelligence.

In this symbiosis, humanity and AGI can co-evolve toward a future that is not only technologically capable but also deeply meaningful. We must embrace our role as meaning-makers, recognizing that our lived experiences, emotions, and narratives are irreplaceable in shaping the trajectory of this new era. Book 4 will introduce a new theory of emotions that can be put to practical use for self-awareness and decision-making, providing a framework for guiding your evolution in ways that honor the richness of your human experience while tapping into the boundless potential of what’s possible.
