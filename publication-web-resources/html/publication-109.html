THE ALIGNMENT ECONOMY

219

This system, far from encouraging passivity, promotes a society where individuals are empowered to make meaningful contributions, guided by a shared understanding of Freedom’s multifaceted nature. Instead of being driven solely by economic necessity, individuals would be free to explore their unique potential, engaging in activities that resonate with their values and contribute to a society that is not just prosperous but also just, equitable, and sustainable.

Basic Income can then be viewed as a hyperparameter, adjustable according to diverse cultural values. Whether set at zero or a higher level, it serves as a baseline within a broader ecosystem of negative prices, acknowledging the inherent value of being alive without requiring granular calculations.

The Alignment Economy, by integrating negative prices, recogniz-ing the inherent value of human existence, and accounting for positive externalities, transcends the limitations of traditional econom-ic models. It envisions a world where abundance is not merely mate-rial, but a reflection of our collective commitment to a freer, more ful-filling future—a world where the currency of Freedom is not just money, but the very act of living a meaningful life.
