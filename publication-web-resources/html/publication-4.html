We’re in the middle of what will perhaps be recognized as the most important decade of human history. The air crackles with anticipation, a sense of standing on the precipice of a new world. Artificial Intelligence, once a futuristic dream, has infiltrated our lives, evolving from narrow applications to the tantalizing possibility of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI). This potential birth of a new intelligence, one that may surpass our own, throws open the doors to a future both exhilarating and terrifying.

This book serves as your guide into this uncharted territory, a map through the complex landscape of the post-AGI world. We will dive into the philosophical dilemmas AGI presents, grapple with the economic transformations it may trigger, and explore the potential societal structures that could emerge.

Forget the dystopian visions of robot overlords or the utopian dreams of effortless abundance. This book is about understanding the real challenges and opportunities AGI offers, and most importantly, how we, as humans, can actively shape this future.

INTRODUCTION

We will embark on a journey through four key stages:

The Imperative of a Shared Vision: Escaping the traps of fear-mongering and utopian fantasies, we’ll explore the crucial need for a unifying vision, a guiding principle to navigate the post-AGI landscape.

The Emanation of Freedom: We’ll introduce the concept of Freedom as a potential guiding ideal, one that transcends traditional definitions and encompasses knowledge, ability, benevolence, and vitality.

The Alignment Economy: Diving into the practicalities, we’ll explore the concept of an Alignment Economy where prices reflect alignment with our shared vision, guiding both human and AGI actions towards collective well-being.

An Integrated Future: Finally, we’ll envision a future where AGI is not a separate entity but an integrated part of our society, amplifying our capabilities and furthering our collective journey towards Freedom.

This book is designed to be obsolete. While some books acquire greatness by standing the test of time, impacting people and culture over decades, centuries or millennia. This book attempts a different challenge: meeting a great moment in time heads on.

An integrated narrative seamlessly weaves a focused main narrative with supplementary side windows containing additional context and insights, creating a multi-layered reading experience that reflects our vision for an interconnected future.

The book is not a prediction of the future; it’s an invitation to participate in its co-creation. It’s a call to action, urging you to engage in critical thought, open dialogue, and conscious action. Because the future of AGI is not predetermined, it’s a future we will build together.

Let’s begin this exploration.
