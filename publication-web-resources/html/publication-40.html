THE IMPERATIVE OF A SHARED VISION

81

The dawning era of Superintelligence represents a momentous turning point for our civilization and spe-cies. The advent of AGI will transform nearly every aspect of human civilization—economic, social, technological, geopolitical, and philosophical. How we navigate the ar-rival of this transformative technology will profoundly im-pact our species’ long-term trajectory. Whether AGI proves an extraordinary utopia or an existential risk de-pends on the foresight, wisdom, and prudence with which we prepare for and manage this historic transition.

Superintelligence achieves cross-domain mastery and flexibility, capable of driving rapid, automated growth that could quickly cascade into dominance—an “intelligence explosion.” In this scenario, successive AGI systems would rapidly improve upon themselves, potentially achieving control over human affairs in just a few years. Even more precarious is the possibility that development may progress in secret, fueled by competitive “arms races” among nations or corporations, unrestrained by accountability or oversight. The concentration of extreme capabilities in such an environment would introduce exponential growth and information asymmetry, posing novel risks of power spiraling out of control.
