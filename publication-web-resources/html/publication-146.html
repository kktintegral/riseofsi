My earliest memory goes back to when I was two years old, attending a school with a creative curriculum. I remember my teacher handing me a paintbrush and encouraging me to paint the wall. The experience was captivating—watching how a simple movement of my hand could instantly change my surroundings. With just a single stroke, the wall turned red, and in that moment, I realized I had the power to meaningfully shape the world around me. Looking back, I now recognize this as my first encounter with the sense of agency. This small yet profound moment planted the seeds for my lifelong interest in creativity.

Just as I discovered my ability to change reality with a paintbrush, we now stand before the canvas of our future, with AGI as our brush.

This book has laid the groundwork, providing a foundation for understanding the potential and challenges of the post-AGI world. We’ve explored the need for a shared vision, the concept of Freedom as a guiding principle, the framework of the Alignment Economy, and the possibilities of an integrated future where humanity and advanced AI coexist and thrive together.

The future is not a destination to which we are headed, it’s a reality we are actively creating. We are not mere spectators of technological advancement, but rather key agents in shaping what lies ahead. The Freedom Era is not an inevitability that will simply unfold before us, it is a shared reality we can collectively realize.
