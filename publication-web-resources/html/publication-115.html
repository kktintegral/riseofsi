AGI AS A CATALYST FOR COMMUNICATIVE RATIONALITY AGI, with its capacity for processing vast amounts of information, translating languages, and facilitating communication across geographical and cultural boundaries, has the potential to revitalize the public sphere.

It can help us to overcome barriers to participation, provide access to diverse perspectives, and create spaces for meaningful dialogue and deliberation on a global scale.

Imagine AGI-powered platforms that enable citizens from all walks of life to engage in constructive debates about the future they want to create. These platforms could provide real-time translation, summarize complex arguments, identify common ground, and facilitate the emergence of consensus through a process of reasoned deliberation.

THE ALIGNMENT ECONOMY

231

The transition to a new social contract requires challenging long-held beliefs, embracing new forms of cooperation, and engaging in difficult conversations. However, the potential rewards are immense. By forging a new social contract, guided by the principles of communicative rationality and a commitment to a vibrant public sphere, we can unlock the transformative potential of Superintelligence to build a new world.

What possible worlds could we aspire to create?

1. Embracing Interdependence: The global reach of AGI transcends national borders and traditional power structures. We must recognize our fundamental interdependence and forge new forms of global governance through open dialogue and collaborative decision-making, ensuring that AGI serves the common good of humanity.

2. Empowering Individual Agency: The Alignment Economy relies on individuals making responsible choices aligned with collective well-being. This requires ensuring individuals have the capacity to participate meaningfully in the public sphere, contributing their unique perspectives and shaping the future. This necessitates equitable access to resources, education, and opportunities for engagement.

3. Cultivating Moral Growth: The Alignment Economy is an invitation to moral and social evolution. Aligning prices with our pursuit of Freedom incentivizes actions that promote well-being, sustainability, and justice. This process, fueled by open dialogue in the public sphere, promotes continuous moral growth, empowering individuals to become active participants in shaping a more ethical and fulfilling future.
