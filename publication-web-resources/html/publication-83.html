By integrating these factors, the Alignment Economy creates a pricing mechanism that reflects not just economic value, but also environmental, social, and ethical considerations, guiding us towards a future where our economic choices align with our deepest values and aspirations.

Environmental Impact: Resources used, pollution generat-ed, and recycling potential.

Technological Contribution: How the product advances our collective knowledge and capabilities, expanding the frontiers of what’s possible and improving access to bene-ficial technologies.

Resource Allocation Alignment: How production and distribution align with efficient and equitable use of resources, considering both abundance and scarcity.

Individual and Collective Well-being: The product’s potential to enhance or detract from human flourishing, including its effects on creativity, social connections, and personal growth.
